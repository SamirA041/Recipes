Keema Curry

ingredients

  • Minced Beef
  • oil/ghee
  • chopped tomatoes
  • onions
  • tomato puree
  • water
  • peas
  • spices powders - cumin, coriander, curry powder, garam masala
  • salt & pepper
  • garlic
  • ginger

cooking steps

  1. First of all, we're going to fry the minced (ground) beef with salt, pepper and cumin until golden brown.
  2. Now we're going to saute the onions before adding the spices along with garlic, ginger and tomato puree (paste).
  3. Add in tinned tomatoes then add the cooked mince back into the pan before simmering for 5-6 minutes to thicken the curry.
  4. Add in some frozen peas for the last couple of minutes
  5. Then serve on some rice and top it all off with some fresh coriander.
